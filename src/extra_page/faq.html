Find answers to common questions about GreenMed, our platform, and how we support plant enthusiasts and botanical shops.

What is GreenMed?
GreenMed is a platform dedicated to connecting plant enthusiasts with premium plants and trusted botanical shops. We offer detailed plant information, care tips, and medicines to ensure your plants thrive.
How do I order medicines for my plants?
On our plants page, each plant lists common diseases and recommended medicines. You can select a quantity and click "Add to Cart" to order medicines. Your cart is saved and can be accessed from the profile section.
What types of plants do you offer?
We offer a variety of plants, including tropical and ornamental categories. You can filter plants by category on the Explore Shop page to find the perfect addition to your collection.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team via email at support@greenmed.com, by phone at (123) 456-7890, or visit us at Cuet 123, Chittagong. Check our Contact page for more details.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we ship to select regions. Please contact our support team to confirm if we can deliver to your location.
